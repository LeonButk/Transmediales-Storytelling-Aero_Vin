COOKIE POLICY

Last updated: 15.06.2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.

They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide information about website usage.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation of the website and cannot be disabled.

Examples include:

  • Security cookies
  • Session cookies
  • Load-balancing cookies
  • User preference cookies

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use our website.

These cookies are only activated after your consent.

We use:

  • Google Analytics

Collected information may include:

  • Pages viewed
  • Visit duration
  • Device type
  • Browser information
  • Approximate location

Video Cookies

When viewing embedded YouTube videos, YouTube may place cookies on your device.

These cookies may be used to:

  • Store preferences
  • Measure video performance
  • Provide analytics
  • Improve user experience

These cookies are only activated after consent.

4. Consent Management

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie banner.

You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize cookie preferences

No analytics or video cookies will be activated before consent is obtained.

5. Withdrawing Consent

You can withdraw your consent at any time through the cookie settings available on this website.

Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

6. Managing Cookies in Your Browser

Most web browsers allow you to:

  • Delete cookies
  • Block cookies
  • Receive notifications before cookies are stored

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics
  • YouTube
  • Wix Hosting Services

These providers may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

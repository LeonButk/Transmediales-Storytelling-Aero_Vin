MOMENTUM is designed to combine digital components and nature. With climate change taking center stage, we explore the chances it can provide and the aspects of life influenced by it. Our prime example is viticulture in Denmark.


This transmedia campaign for ÆRØ VIN includes a series of documentaries on the aspects of life influenced by climate change as well as the ÆRØ VIN TRAIL, an interactive wine trail filled with information on viticulture in Denmark.

From Filmmaking to Winemaking

Between reinventing himself and finding his footing on Ærø, Guido Weihermüller takes on a daring project in a foreign country.

A decade ago, life as a winemaker couldn’t have seemed more absurd for Guido. Despite relatives maintaining their own vineyards, Guido had no contact points with winemaking. Today, he operates his own winery. Guido kneels between vines, harvests their grapes and tends to the bio diversity on his vineyard. Reinventing yourself from scratch doesn’t need to be perfect or planned, it just needs to be earnest.

Trading skyscrapers for the sea and countryside sounds like a dream. But Silvia and Guido Weihermüller first had to learn to live in harmony with nature…

"Perfect doesn’t exist. Just get started, step by step."

An Island can Change Everything

Silvia and Guido blossomed with new intensity on Ærø, delving into nature and rediscovering themselves from a new perspective.

In the beginning, the Weihermüllers couldn’t fathom moving from a life in the film industry in a big German city to a quiet life on a small Danish island. Their hectic day-to-day life got paused by corona, showing them nothing is permanent. So why not try something new? Escape the never ending stress of the film industry and start a new life? Together they believed in their cause and found their MOMENTUM on Ærø.

To be on the same page is the most vital thing in a shared life. However, knowing when to accept further help is just as important…

“The quality comes from the vineyard, our cellar work just refines it.”

The Mentor of Danish Wines

A friend’s guiding hand may be the first step to success.

Ben Viehl, owner of the wine estate Skærsøgaard Vin, took on the mantle of Guido's mentor. He opens up about his passion to help other winemakers and shines light on his relationship with his friend Guido. Both had their own struggles with finding their footing in the world of wine. Ben draws a comparison between himself and Guido stepping foot in Denmark and discovering their vineyards potential.

“There is pressure to act, I have to make a difference myself. I can’t just stand by and let things carry on as they are.”

Climate Sailor

About climate sailing and the importance of pioneer work

Climate Sailor Matthias Berg is used to constantly adapting to new circumstances on short notice. After all, the sea must be listened to. With climate change, it’s much the same, although society rarely reacts on time. Between his life on deck and activism towards climate change on land, Matthias draws a comparison between the ever-changing sea and environmental change. Part of his work on the subject of climate is installing weather stations. These help gather data to improve agriculture, such as the vineyard of his friends Guido and Silvia. According to Matthias, climate and nature must be understood, not controlled.

Not letting yourself get distracted by the idyllic landscapes and your own astonishment is particularly important when working on the topic of climate on vineyards…

“[Danish winemakers] had this pioneering spirit, everything was unorganized, it was adventurous, they had grape varieties nobody had heard of.”

Wine meets Cool Climate

An insight in the concept of cool climate wine and the opportunity of viticulture in Denmark

Just a few decades ago, wineries and especially vineyards in Denmark seemed unthinkable. Today, Denmark is one of the EUs official wine-growing regions. Cool climate expert Jan Eggers offers an insight in the importance of choosing the right location and speaks about first wine-growing experiments and the history of the cool climate. In his line of work, Jan visits a lot of vineyards. During those he offers opinions and advice to newer winemakers, one of them being Guido.

Sweat, joy and struggles are all part of a winemaker's life. When it comes to wine, the passion to create your own product becomes tangible for everyone…

“Everyone has the chance to take something with them that has a meaning to them.”

Get your own MOMENTUM

Going above and beyond to find yourself anew.

Guido and Silvia Weihermüller found their MOMENTUM on Ærø. Their new life, complete with house, garden and winery, sparks joy in their hearts. According to them, this feeling must be shared with the world. Today, ÆRØ VINs vineyard provides a space for everyone to find their own MOMENTUM. Of course, this is a lot of work for the two of them. However, both experience and outcome prove it worthwhile.

MOMENTUM isn’t just about climate change. It’s about rediscovering yourself. Taking opportunities. Finding a new perspective.


We want you to feel inspired to find your own MOMENTUM.

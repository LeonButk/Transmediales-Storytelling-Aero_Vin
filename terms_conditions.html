2. Wine Tastings

2.1 Reservations

Wine tasting reservations may be requested by email or through any contact method provided by the winery.

Reservations become binding only after written confirmation has been issued by the winery.

The winery reserves the right to refuse reservation requests based on availability, operational requirements, or other reasonable circumstances.

2.2 Payment

Unless otherwise specified, payment for wine tastings is made on-site on the day of the visit.

Accepted payment methods may include:

  • Credit and debit cards
  • MobilePay
  • Cash (where accepted)
  • Other approved payment methods

2.3 Wine Tastings with Food Pairings

For wine tastings that include food pairings, cheese boards, meals, snacks, or other catering services, advance payment for the food portion is required.

A reservation involving food is only considered confirmed once the required prepayment has been received.

Payment instructions will be provided upon confirmation of the reservation.

The winery reserves the right to cancel unconfirmed reservations if payment is not received within the requested timeframe.

2.4 Walk-In Guests

Walk-in visitors are welcome whenever capacity allows.

However, guests holding confirmed reservations will be given priority.

Availability for walk-in tastings cannot be guaranteed, particularly during weekends, holidays, harvest season, and special events.

Advance reservations are therefore strongly recommended.

2.5 Cancellation by Guests

Wine Tastings Without Prepaid Food

Guests are kindly requested to notify the winery as soon as possible if they are unable to attend.

Wine Tastings With Prepaid Food

  • More than 7 days before the tasting: full refund of prepaid food arrangements.
  • Between 3 and 7 days before the tasting: 50% refund.
  • Less than 72 hours before the tasting: no refund.

At its discretion, the winery may offer an alternative date instead of a refund.

2.6 Cancellation by the Winery

The winery reserves the right to cancel, postpone, or modify wine tastings due to:

  • Illness
  • Severe weather conditions
  • Operational issues
  • Force majeure events
  • Insufficient participation
  • Circumstances beyond reasonable control

Any prepaid amounts will be refunded in full or transferred to a new date by mutual agreement.

3. Vineyard Tours

3.1 Reservations

Vineyard tours may be reserved in advance via email or other designated booking channels.

Advance reservations are recommended due to limited capacity.

3.2 Payment and Confirmation

A vineyard tour reservation becomes valid only after full payment has been received.

Payment may be made via:

  • MobilePay
  • Bank transfer
  • Other payment methods approved by the winery

Payment instructions will be provided during the booking process.

Unpaid reservations may be cancelled without further notice.

3.3 Guest Responsibility

Vineyard tours take place outdoors on agricultural land.

Participants are responsible for wearing suitable clothing and footwear appropriate for weather conditions and uneven terrain.

Guests participate at their own risk.

The winery accepts no responsibility for discomfort, injury, or inconvenience resulting from inappropriate clothing or footwear.

3.4 Cancellation by Guests

  • More than 14 days before the tour: full refund.
  • Between 7 and 14 days before the tour: 50% refund.
  • Less than 7 days before the tour: no refund.

The winery may, at its discretion, offer rescheduling where practical.

3.5 Weather Conditions

Vineyard tours generally take place regardless of normal weather conditions.

In cases of severe weather or safety concerns, the winery reserves the right to:

  • Modify the route
  • Shorten the tour
  • Reschedule the tour
  • Cancel the tour

If cancelled by the winery, participants may choose either a full refund or an alternative tour date.

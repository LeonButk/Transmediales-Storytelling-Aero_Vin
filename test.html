MOMENTUM

scrollen

The campaign MOMENTUM is designed to combine digital components and nature. With climate change taking center stage, we explore the chances it can provide and the aspects of life influenced by it. Our prime example is viticulture in Denmark.

This is a project from us students in the degree program Digitales Storytelling from Ostfalia University of Applied Sciences, Germany. It is part of the course transmedia storytelling in the fourth semester. We produced a series of documentaries on the aspects of life influenced by climate change, including both life at sea and on land. On top of that, we also brought to life a wine trail under the name ÆRØ VIN TRAIL, filled with interactive information, both physically and digitally, on viticulture in Denmark.

MOMENTUM is designed to combine digital components and nature. With climate change taking center stage, we explore the chances it can provide and the aspects of life influenced by it. Our prime example is viticulture in Denmark.

This transmedia campaign for ÆRØ VIN includes a series of documentaries on the aspects of life influenced by climate change, including both life at sea and on land. On top of that, we also brought to life a wine trail under the name ÆRØ VIN TRAIL, filled with interactive information, both physically and digitally, on viticulture in Denmark.

In the beginning, the Weihermüllers couldn’t fathom moving from a life in the film industry in a big German city to a quiet life on a small Danish island. Corona showed them nothing is permanent nor secure. Why not try something completely different? Away from the never ending stress the film industry put on them? Why not start a new life? Their house, their garden, their plot and eventually their vineyard are all part of their own MOMENTUM. Together they believed in their cause. “What’s important is having someone who believes with you.”

From Filmmaking to Winemaking

Just a few decades ago, wineries and especially vineyards in Denmark seemed unthinkable. Today, Denmark is one of the EUs official wine-growing regions. Cool climate expert Jan Eggers explains why the climate in Denmark is so special and how wine-growing is possible in this cooler region. He offers an insight in the importance of choosing the right location, the special effect the proximity to the sea offers, speaks about first wine-growing experiments and the history of the cool climate. In his line of work, Jan visits a lot of vineyards. There he offers both opinions and advice to other winemakers. Building connections through their shared love of wine is very dear to him. Guido is one of the winemakers profiting from Jan's visit.
